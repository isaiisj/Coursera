Exploring the HyperText Transfer Protocol

You are to retrieve the following document in a browser
tab with "Developer Mode" enabled so you can examine the
HTTP Response headers. 
You may need to open the URL in a new tab, turn on developer mode,
and then press refresh to get the proper data.

http://data.pr4e.org/intro-short.txt
Enter the header values:

- Last-Modified
- ETag
- Content-Length
- Cache-Control
- Content-Type
